Tools for Heroes

At L.N. Curtis & sons we believe in delivering fire fighting products and services in a way that exemplifies our core values of Quality, Service, Integrity and Caring. Outstanding customer service is a top priority for every team member.

We consider our customers as partners, and endeavor to establish long-term relationships based on education, quality fire rescue products and services, and ethical behavior.

Whenever the call comes, you respond selflessly with focus, and determination to get the job done quickly and safely. You educate and train so that when the time comes, you will be ready. We consider what you do to be the work of heroes.

We are dedicated and committed to providing you, our hero customer, with high quality, integrity and service in every interaction.

We care about our customers, our team members, our suppliers and our communities; we have been this way since 1929.
